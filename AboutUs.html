About Us

Welcome to Hair_by_thembii. We are located in Orlando West, Soweto. We specialize in African Styles Braid.

Our regular hours of operation are Tue.-Sat. from 8am to 7pm, and also Sundays from 10am to 6pm.

Our dedication to our clients has always been to deliver the best-looking, high-quality braids, featuring a variety of styles you can choose from, resulting from years of a growing number of happy clients.

Because preserving the culture and fashion trends in our industry is very important, Hair_by_thembii employs highly-skilled and experienced young hair braiders with the ultimate goal to keep you, your friends, and your family feeling comfortable and beautiful.

Hair_by_thembii provides multiple hair services such as Micro Braids, Corn Rows, Invisible Locks, Twists, and many more. These familiar styles are expressly deployed all over the world.

By using efficient braiding techniques and hair specialists, our clients are able to save time and money.

We sincerely thank you, our many patrons, for supporting us throughout all these months and years. Please visit our gallery today and pick a style that is suitable for you, or your family and friends.

See you soon.

~ Ms. Thembekile Mokoena
